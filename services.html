Our Services

Treatment Programs we have

Alcohol addiction

Addiction can have a profound impact on families, affecting relationships, communication, and overall well-being. TTRC offers family therapy and education programs to support loved ones in understanding and coping with their family member's challenges.

Contact Us

Trauma

Police officers, firefighters, paramedics, and other first responders often encounter traumatic events in the line of duty, leading to increased risk of substance abuse and mental health issues. Our programs offer specialized support tailored to the needs of first responders, including trauma-informed care and peer support.

Contact Us

Mental illness

mental health disorders is a problem in out community today. at TTRC our integrated treatment approach addresses the complex interplay between amental illness, providing diagnosis treatment to promote holistic healing.

PTSD

TTRC is proud to provide specialized treatment for personel struggling with substance abuse or mental health disorders. Our programs address the unique challenges faced by veterans, including PTSD, trauma, and reintegration into civilian life.

Meditation

Mindfulness-based meditation practices help individuals cultivate inner peace, reduce stress, and enhance self-awareness. At TTRC we offer a peaceful environment for meditation and offer programs to help individual.

Group Therapy

Multi-family group therapy brings together multiple families facing similar challenges to share experiences, provide mutual support, and learn from one another. It fosters a sense of community, reduces feelings of isolation, and promotes collaborative problem-solving.
